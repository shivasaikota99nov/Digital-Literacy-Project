# Stay Safe Online: Student Checklist

- Never enter your UPI PIN to "receive" money, as PINs are only required for sending funds.
- Verify the recipient’s name on your screen after scanning any QR code to avoid payment tampering.
- Use a VPN or mobile hotspot instead of public Wi-Fi when accessing sensitive university portals.
- Enable biometric authentication for your UPI and Gmail apps to add a layer of security beyond SMS OTPs.
- Revoke unnecessary app permissions for "Location" or "Contacts" in your phone’s settings every few months
- Use a secondary email address for social media and contest sign-ups to protect your primary student account.
- Ignore unsolicited messages offering "scholarships" or "jobs" that ask for an upfront registration fee.
- Report any digital fraud immediately to the National Cyber Crime Portal at cybercrime.gov.in or by calling the 1930 Helpline.
